# Windhoek Attractions & Places of Interest

## Museums
1. Windhoek City Museum - City history and culture
2. TransNamib Museum (Bahnhof Street) - Railway history
3. Erfdeel Museum - Heritage museum
4. Independence Memorial Museum - Opened March 2014, independence history
5. National Art Gallery (NAGN) - Namibian art
6. National Earth Science Museum - Geology and earth sciences
7. Old Wheeler's Museum - Historical artifacts

## Historic Buildings
1. Windhoek Central Railway Station - Historic architecture
2. Old Prison in Goethe Street - Colonial era prison
3. College of the Arts (Fidel Castro Street) - Since 1894
4. Turnhalle Building (Bahnhof Street) - Built 1909
5. Old Brewery (Sam Nujoma Ave & Tal Street) - Historic brewery
6. Tintenpalast (Robert Mugabe Avenue) - Built 1912, Parliament
7. Elizabeth House (Polytechnic) - Built 1907
8. Alte Feste - One of oldest buildings in Windhoek

## Churches
1. Christuskirche - Oldest Evangelical Lutheran church
2. Roman Catholic Cathedral
3. Catholic Mission Church - Plans completed 1915

## Memorials & Cemeteries
1. Heroes' Acre - Modern memorial
2. Commonwealth War Graves
3. Old Gammams Cemetery
4. Old Location Cemetery (1924-1960)

## Nature & Parks
- Tintenpalast Gardens - Popular picnic spot
- Daan Viljoen Nature Reserve - Game viewing
- Avis Dam Nature Reserve
- Zoo Park
- National Botanical Gardens

## Popular Activities
- City walking tours
- Township tours (Katutura)
- Craft markets (Namibia Craft Centre)
- Restaurant scene (Joe's Beerhouse, Stellenbosch Wine Bar)
- Day trips to nearby game reserves
