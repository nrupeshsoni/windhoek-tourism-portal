# Namibia Routes Research

## Key Findings from Tourism Sources

### Popular Route Durations
- 7 days: Minimum for highlights
- 10 days: Average road trip duration
- 14 days: Perfect length for main attractions
- 21-30 days: Complete country exploration

### Classic 10-Day Route (Windhoek Circuit)
**Day 1**: Windhoek - Arrive, explore capital
**Day 2**: Windhoek → Sesriem/Sossusvlei (350km) - Desert scenery
**Day 3**: Sossusvlei/Deadvlei - Iconic red dunes, dead trees
**Day 4**: Sesriem → Swakopmund (350km) - Coastal town
**Day 5**: Swakopmund - Adventure activities, German architecture
**Day 6**: Swakopmund → Damaraland (300km) - Desert elephants, Twyfelfontein
**Day 7**: Damaraland → Etosha (250km) - Enter national park
**Day 8**: Etosha National Park - Safari, waterholes
**Day 9**: Etosha National Park - More wildlife viewing
**Day 10**: Etosha → Windhoek (414km) - Return

### Scenic Roads
1. C12/C14: Fish River Canyon to Swakopmund - Desert scenery
2. C34: Swakopmund along Skeleton Coast - Shipwrecks, seals
3. B8: Caprivi Strip - Elephants near road

### Best Seasons
- Peak Season: May-September (dry, cooler)
- Green Season: November-April (landscapes, fewer tourists)
- Wildlife: Dry season best for Etosha
