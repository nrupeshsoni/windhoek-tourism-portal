# Comprehensive Namibia Tourism Content

## Key Facts About Namibia
- Houses the oldest desert in the world (Namib Desert - 55-80 million years old)
- Only desert where you can find large mammals like elephants, rhinos, lions and giraffes
- Highest sand dunes in the world at Sossusvlei (over 300m)
- Second largest canyon in the world - Fish River Canyon
- 14 diverse regions with unique landscapes and cultures
- Total area: 824,292 km²
- Population: ~2.5 million
- 11 official languages

## The 14 Regions of Namibia

### 1. Erongo Region
- **Capital:** Swakopmund
- **Population:** ~150,000
- **Area:** 63,719 km²
- **Climate:** Coastal Desert
- **Best Time:** Year-round
- **Highlights:** Sandboarding, Dolphin Cruises, German Architecture, Coastal Activities
- **Description:** Coastal paradise where the Namib Desert meets the Atlantic Ocean. Home to Swakopmund and adventure capital.

### 2. Khomas Region
- **Capital:** Windhoek
- **Population:** ~400,000
- **Area:** 36,804 km²
- **Climate:** Semi-Arid
- **Best Time:** Year-round
- **Highlights:** City Tours, Museums, Fine Dining, Shopping
- **Description:** The political and economic heart of Namibia, featuring the capital city and modern amenities.

### 3. Otjozondjupa Region
- **Capital:** Otjiwarongo
- **Population:** ~145,000
- **Area:** 105,460 km²
- **Climate:** Semi-Arid
- **Best Time:** May - October
- **Highlights:** Game Drives, Wildlife Photography, Big Five, Bird Watching
- **Description:** Home to Etosha National Park, Namibia's premier wildlife destination with incredible game viewing.

### 4. Hardap Region
- **Capital:** Mariental
- **Population:** ~79,000
- **Area:** 109,651 km²
- **Climate:** Arid Desert
- **Best Time:** March - October
- **Highlights:** Dune Climbing, Photography, Desert Tours, Hot Air Ballooning
- **Description:** Home to Sossusvlei and the world's most spectacular desert landscapes and red sand dunes.

### 5. Kunene Region
- **Capital:** Opuwo
- **Population:** ~90,000
- **Area:** 115,293 km²
- **Climate:** Arid Desert
- **Best Time:** May - October
- **Highlights:** Cultural Tours, Desert Wildlife, Photography, Adventure Tours
- **Description:** Remote wilderness region featuring Himba culture, desert elephants, and the dramatic Skeleton Coast.

### 6. Karas Region
- **Capital:** Keetmanshoop
- **Population:** ~77,000
- **Area:** 161,514 km²
- **Climate:** Arid
- **Best Time:** March - October
- **Highlights:** Canyon Hiking, Ghost Towns, Colonial Architecture, Coastal Tours
- **Description:** Southern region featuring Fish River Canyon, diamond coast, and fascinating colonial history.

### 7. Oshana Region
- **Capital:** Oshakati
- **Highlights:** Traditional Markets, Local Culture
- **Description:** Northern region with vibrant local culture, traditional markets, and gateway to Angola.

### 8. Oshikoto Region
- **Capital:** Omuthiya
- **Highlights:** Lake Otjikoto, Traditional Villages
- **Description:** Northern region known for Lake Otjikoto, traditional culture, and historical sites.

### 9. Ohangwena Region
- **Capital:** Eenhana
- **Highlights:** Traditional Culture, Agricultural Areas
- **Description:** Northern border region with traditional Owambo culture and rural agricultural communities.

### 10. Omusati Region
- **Capital:** Outapi
- **Highlights:** Traditional Culture, Agricultural Heritage
- **Description:** Northwestern region with traditional Owambo culture and agricultural heritage.

### 11. Zambezi Region (formerly Caprivi Strip)
- **Capital:** Katima Mulilo
- **Highlights:** Zambezi River, Tropical Climate
- **Description:** Tropical northeastern region with river systems, lush vegetation, and unique ecosystem.

### 12. Kavango East Region
- **Capital:** Rundu
- **Highlights:** Kavango River, Traditional Culture
- **Description:** Eastern region along the Kavango River with traditional culture and river-based activities.

### 13. Kavango West Region
- **Capital:** Nkurenkuru
- **Highlights:** River Culture, Traditional Communities
- **Description:** Western Kavango region with river culture, traditional communities, and craft traditions.

### 14. Omaheke Region
- **Capital:** Gobabis
- **Highlights:** Cattle Farming, Kalahari Desert
- **Description:** Eastern region known for cattle farming, Kalahari ecosystem, and San cultural heritage.

## Practical Travel Information
- **Emergency Numbers:** Police: 10111 | Medical: 061-203-9111 | Tourism Hotline: 061-290-6000
- **Currency:** Namibian Dollar (NAD), South African Rand also accepted
- **Languages:** English (official), Afrikaans, German, Oshiwambo, Herero, Nama/Damara
- **Driving:** Left-hand side, international license required
- **Visa:** Many nationalities get visa on arrival (90 days)
- **Health:** Malaria prophylaxis recommended for northern regions
- **Best Time to Visit:** May-October (dry season) for wildlife; Year-round for landscapes

## When to Visit
- **Dry Season (May-October):** Best for wildlife viewing, cooler temperatures
- **Green Season (November-April):** Lush landscapes, bird watching, lower prices
- **Peak Season (July-October):** Best weather, busiest period
- **Shoulder Season (April-May, November):** Good balance of weather and fewer crowds
